If you're sick and tired of the same old corporate promises like me, well then you've come to the right place. Here at Stilt Fox™ we can't lose your data because we don't collect it. We believe in giving you control of your life again. This means storing files on your local machines, offering conveniences only previously seen online, and revitalizing the boxed software market. We believe that when you buy a product you own that copy. No more leasing, no more borrowing, no more paying rent, this software is yours to use as you see fit.

About Stilt Fox™

Stilt Fox™ is focused on creating software products that give users control over their digital life. We believe that as big tech draws farther and farther away from public interest that software alternatives need to be offered, both paid and non-paid. We feel that customers and products should be the focus, not data collection and advertisement. We're here to cut the fancy sale's pitches and jargon to offer focused and concise technical experiences. It's time to take a deep breath and come down from the cloud.

What we mean by customer focused

To us, customer focus means that we should not engage in predatory anti-consumer sales tactics. some examples include:

  • Data collection of any type
  • Micro-transaction laden phone applications
  • Use of FOMO (the fear of missing out)
  • Subscriptions for EVERYTHING
  • Excessive use of Corporate lingo to confuse customers
  • Using cloud to enforce lack of ownership of purchased goods
If, like us, you are sick of this kind of nonsense then we hope our products will offer you a breath of fresh air.

Supporting the Cause
